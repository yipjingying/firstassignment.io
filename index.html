A DAY TRIP IN PULAU UBIN

-Singapore's Favorite Island-



FOR THE NATURE LOVERS...

🌿 Sensory Trail

Immerse yourself in tranquility, take a short 1.5km stroll in this flat trail. Find out more about the different plants such as basil, turmeric and curry leaf tree. Not only that, observe the native plants and fruit trees on this island! Do keep a lookout for the Pulau Ubin Tree that is right by the Sensory Trail route, it makes a great place to rest and picnic!


🌊 Pekan Quarry Viewpoint

A 15-minute walk/3 minute cycle from the jetty will bring you to a beautiful site at the Pekan Quarry. Don't forget your cameras if you are planning to take insta-worthy shots here at the Quarry.


🦋 Butterfly Hill

The Butterfly Hill houses more than 140 types of butterflies. We challenge you to find and snap a picture of the rare Dwarf Crow which was thought to be extinct but rediscovered in 2002!
Opening Hours: 24 hours


FOR THE ACTIVE ONES...

🚵 Ketam Mountain Bike Park

Attention to all Mountain bikers! Cycle through Ubin's biking trails which has three different tracks, Blue Square, Black Diamond & Double Black diamond. The 3 tracks have difficulty levels ranging from difficult to extremely difficult. Remember to bring your water bottles because this activity is going to be a fun yet sweaty ride!


🚣 Pulau Ubin Mangrove Kayaking Adventure

Gather your friends (Not more than 8 of course) and embark on a Kayak adventure in Pulau Ubin Mangrove. Put your Rediscovering Singapore Vouvhers to good use now by booking your trip with Klook now!


🚲 Rent a bicycle

For those who wants leisure activities, you can rent a bicycle in Pulau Ubin at low rates! Travel around the island with ease and enjoy the scenic views today. If you are going during peak periods, do consider renting your bicycles online with Klook before your trip!


FOR THE HUNGRY ONES...

🍽 Encik Hassan's shop

Makan in one of Pulau Ubin's oldest restaurant that is located right next to the Ubin Jetty! The shop sells Malay cuisines such as Mee Rebus, Nasi Lemak and more. Do check it out when you're in Ubin!


🐠 Cheong Lian Yuen

Craving for seafood? Cheong Lian Yuen got you covered! Located beside the Ubin Jetty, the restaurant serves seafood with a dining area in front of its shop.


🍵 Sin Nam Huat

Runned by two sisters opposite the Wayang Stage, Sin Nam Huat served traditional coffee and tea, and seafood too! Look at how Ubin was like in the past through the pictures on the walls of Sin Nam Huat.
